Matcha Mousse Cake

Ingredients

35g Crushed Lotus Cookies

15g Melted unsalted butter

25g White chocolate  

25g Hot milk 

1g Gelatin sheet (or Powdered gelatin)

Vanilla bean paste

40g Whipped cream (whipped 50%)

30g Egg yolk  

15g Sugar 

60g Hot milk 

15g White chocolate 

4g Gelatin sheet (or 3g Powdered gelatin)

3g Green tea powder

135g Whipped cream (whipped 50%)

10g Hot whipping cream  

2g Green tea powder

30g White chocolate

7g Corn syrup

1g Gelatin sheet (or Powdered gelatin)

  1.  Mix crusty cookies and melted butter and put them on the bottom of the mold and put them in the fridge.
  2. 2Add white chocolate to hot milk and mix. Add gelatin and vanilla paste to cold water for at least 10 minutes and mix.
  3.  Add 50% whipped cream into the hemp mold and mix in the freezer for about 1 hour.
  4. Mix egg yolk and sugar in a pan, mix hot milk little by little, add to the fire, and heat until lightly thick.
  5. Add white chocolate,mix together, then mix green tea powder.
  6. Add 50% of whipped cream into the whipped cream, mix in a little, and let stand in the freezer for about 10 minutes.
  7. Put a hemispherical white chocolate mousse on a firm mousse, pour the green tea mousse cream over the bongum mousse, and put one more bengal mousse on it.
  8. Pour the mousse according to the height of the frame, flatten it and let it harden for about 2 hours in the fridge.
  9. Put green tea powder in hot cream and mix.
  10. Place a hot pot of white chocolate in a pot of about 50 degrees, dissolve the chocolate (9), add the starch syrup, add the baked gelatin and mix.
  11. It squeezes into the sieve, puts it in the pocket, and cools it down on the firm mousse.
  12. Enjoy!
